About Our Jasmine Pearl Tea

Our Jasmine Pearl Green Tea is a masterpiece of tea craftsmanship. Each pearl is carefully hand-rolled by skilled artisans in the Fujian province of China, using only the finest spring-picked green tea leaves.

The tea undergoes a traditional scenting process where it's layered with fresh jasmine blossoms seven times over several weeks. This labor-intensive method ensures the tea absorbs the maximum amount of jasmine fragrance without artificial flavors.

When brewed, the pearls slowly unfurl, releasing a sweet floral aroma and producing a light golden liquor with a smooth, slightly sweet taste and lingering floral finish.
